## How Pro overage is calculated

**MAU** = distinct users who authenticated at least once in the current billing period. The period is monthly, anchored on the day of your first Pro payment.

```
If you end the period with 130,000 MAU:
  100,000 included in the flat ($29)
  30,000 above × $0.02 = $600 overage

Total billable: $29 + $600 = $629
```

**Spending cap**: configurable. When projection would cross the cap, we block new signups. Your bill never goes above the cap. [How to set it →](/en/dashboard/billing/#spending-cap)

## Questions we get

**Do you charge per workspace or per developer?**
Per workspace. Your whole team accesses the same workspace at no extra cost.

**Do you charge for admin users?**
No. Workspace members (the humans on *your* team in `app.prysmid.com`) are unlimited on all plans.

**Annual discount?**
On Pro no (it's flat cheap). On Enterprise yes, negotiable.

**Do you accept invoice / wire transfer?**
Pro: card via Stripe only. Enterprise: invoice + wire available.

**LATAM tax?**
Yes, applicable VAT included on the invoice per country (Stripe handles it).

**What if I stop paying?**
14 days `past_due` grace, then `suspended` (user login disabled). Recoverable: pay and it's back. Data is not deleted until 90 days after `suspended`.

**Refunds?**
No automatic refund. On cancellation, cancel-at-period-end (you keep using until period end). For special cases, talk to support.

## Plan changes

- **Free → Pro**: [Upgrade in dashboard](/en/dashboard/billing/), prorated from the day.
- **Pro → Free**: Schedule at period end. No refund for current period. Cancelable up to the day before.
- **Pro → Enterprise**: Talk to sales, manual migration.
- **Enterprise → Pro**: Negotiated contractually.
