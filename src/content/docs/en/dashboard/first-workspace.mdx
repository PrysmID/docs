title: Your first workspace
description: What happens during the 60-90s post-signup. What to do when provisioning ends.
---

After your first login on `app.prysmid.com`, we ask for:

1. **Workspace slug** (e.g. `acme`). Immutable. Pick it thinking about *your product*, not your team.
2. **Initial plan** (Free by default; you can jump to Pro here or later).

Click "Create" and provisioning begins. For the next 60-90 seconds we do:

| Step | Takes | What it does |
|---|---|---|
| Reserve slug | `<1s` | Verify `acme` isn't in use. |
| Create DNS | 5-10s | `auth.acme.prysmid.com` → CNAME to the instance. Cloudflare Records API. |
| Provision Zitadel | 30-60s | Spin up dedicated instance, Postgres schema, JWKS keys. |
| Configure SMTP | 5-10s | Connect the instance to EmBoux (transactional email service). |
| Default branding | `<1s` | "Powered by Prysm:ID" logo + default palette. |
| TLS cert | 10-30s | Let's Encrypt for `auth.acme.prysmid.com`. |
| Healthcheck | `<5s` | Validate the OIDC endpoint responds. |
| Flip to `active` | `<1s` | Done. |

While provisioning you'll see a screen with a **live stepper** showing which step is running. You don't have to wait staring at it — you can close the tab and come back. When it finishes, you get an email "Your workspace is ready".

## If provisioning fails

It does sometimes — usually slow Cloudflare DNS, long Zitadel cold start, or EmBoux SMTP quota. When it happens, the workspace stays at `failed_provisioning` with a detail of the failed step:

- **Recoverable**: click "Retry provisioning" on the error screen. Slug is preserved.
- **If it fails again on retry**: write to support. Almost always new-account config (initial quotas) we resolve manually.

## After `active`

Three things to do in order:

1. **Register your first OAuth app.** [Quickstart step 3](/en/quickstart/#the-walkthrough). You get `client_id` and `client_secret` for your code.
2. **Invite your team.** Settings → Members → Invite. Send the link, they sign up at `app.prysmid.com` and join the workspace.
3. **Connect at least one social IdP** ([Google](/en/dashboard/idps/) is the first reflex). If you don't, your users can only sign up with email + password — perfectly valid but lower conversion.

## After that

Depends on your product:

- B2B with enterprise customers → read [/integrate/your-saas-app](/en/integrate/your-saas-app/) and model tenants before you have real customers.
- Your product ships with its own AI agent → connect Claude via MCP and let it configure the rest.
- 6-month plan is upgrade to Pro → bookmark [/reference/pricing](/en/reference/pricing/) and set your spending cap before crossing 100k MAU.
